ACCORDIONS ARE AWESOME

And here's just a FEW reasons why.

    • COOL PEOPLE PLAY THEM.
      • Weird Al Yankovic (of Weird Al)
      • Neil Cicierega (of Lemon Demon)
      • John Linnell (of They Might Be Giants)
      • Martin Molin (of Wintergatan)
      • Krist Novoselic (of Nirvana)
      • ME! (me)

    • THEY'RE THE MOST STEAMPUNK INSTRUMENT.
      • Rows of exciting, mysteriously unlabeled buttons/switches!
      • Attached to your body with leather straps
      • Full of metal levers and valves
      • Powered by pumping bellows
      • Impractically heavy
      • SHINY

    • THEY'RE UNIQUE !
      • Unless you have some really cool friends, then you'll be the only person you know who plays one!

    • INCREDIBLY VERSATILE.
      • All the crunchy, textural chord power of a guitar with the music-theory ease of a keyboard.
      • Dynamic volume control, baby.
      • Play bass and treble lines at the same time!
      • Use register switches to completely change the instrument's sound!
      • Carry the sound of a pipe organ in a mobile squeezebox!
      • AND you can sing along while playing! Really, what more do you want out of an instrument?

    • NOBODY WILL STEAL IT.
      • They're clunky, conspicuous, and unwanted by the general population.
      • (Okay, they sometimes get stolen, but it's pretty easy to spot a thief.)
