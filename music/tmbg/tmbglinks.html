TMBG Old Web Directory

In building my website, I fell in love with the web 1.0 look and creation ethic, and spent quite a lot of time on sites/pages from TMBG fans of years past. Here's some of the best-preserved. (Real oddities marked with ??)


Live sites no longer maintained:

  • Biff's TMBG Stuff (Some pics, and MIDI for TMBG, State Songs, and Mono Puff.)
  • TMBG.org (Band info, some strange downloadable games.)
  • Wompedy TMBG (TMBG-themed font downloads, tiled backgrounds, info.)
  • Bryceland's TMBG page (Sapphire Bullets fanpage, printable home-burned CD inserts, couple of early DAS recordings)
  • Mono Puff website
  • TDK's Mono Puff Syndicate Shrine
  • Schmitty's TMBG fanpage (click through to get to a page with a bunch of cartoons, mostly fanart)

  • alt.music.tmbg messageboard (Searchable back until 1996. I hope early 2000s user Bibliophilia writes a book someday. I'm enthralled by her outbursts of charmingly graphic biological prose.)
