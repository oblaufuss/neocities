MEET JAMES ENSOR

Some talented, interesting visual artists to check out if you're into TMBG.

Tracy Jacks

Connection: Big fan and frequent fabric fanart-er

Links: Tumblr / Instagram

Colorful, embroidered, neurotic dolls and lyric/slogan patches.

Hine Mizushima

Connection: Fan whose stop-motion Ana Ng video led to official commissions from the band.

Links: Website / Etsy

Bright, childlike fabric and needlework depicting friendly insects, cephalopods, and nautical/anatomical themes galore.

Katie Kimmel

Connection: Creator of Dan Miller's baffling CHICKEN PARM T-shirt.

Links: Website / Instagram

Clothing and ceramics with a slightly surreal food theme. Almost Jack-Stauber-ish.

Joren Cull

Connection: Animator of the I Like Fun video and artist on several bits of 2018 merch.

Links: Website / Instagram

Wobbly, doofy pastel illustrations of animals and people.
