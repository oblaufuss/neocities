>>>>> FAN #3: Me! <<<<<

I first found out about the Chessmaster April 2018, wrote the TMBW page on it, and fell in love with it enough that I decided I needed my own. So I made one out of what I had: cardboard, spray paint, and a whole lotta hot glue. Obviously it's not functional, but I can't play guitar and I'm not a lefty, so who cares.

I also really wanted a keychain (come on, TMBG merch store, get on this!) so when I had access to a laser cutter October 2018 I made my own.


I made lots, so I sent some to friends. The mini Chessmasters now have homes in Maine, Massachusetts, Michigan, Iowa, and the Netherlands!

I may still have some spares... email me if you're interested!
